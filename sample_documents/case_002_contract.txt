  the  total  enterprise  value  of  the  transaction,  as  mutually  agreed  in  writing
  at  closing,  but  in  no  event  less  than  FIVE  HUNDRED  THOUSAND  DOLLARS
  ($500,000)  nor  more  than  TWO  MILLION  FIVE  HUNDRED  THOUSAND  DOLLARS
  ($2,500,000).

  pocket  expenses  within  thirty  (30)  days  of  receipt  of  itemized  invoices.
